
# Holiday and Observance Calendar

This calendar consolidates commonly used observances from the Angry Monkey Docs holiday source into a planning-friendly structure.

## Calendar Strategy

- Plan greeting and awareness content at least 2 to 4 weeks in advance.
- Split observances into campaign types: branding, awareness, engagement, and conversion.
- Avoid repetitive posting patterns by rotating observance categories.

## Monthly Planning Themes

### January

- New Year campaigns and annual kickoffs.
- Technology and productivity themes.
- Customer engagement moments.

### February

- Relationship-centered campaigns.
- Wellness and awareness initiatives.
- Short seasonal promotions.

### March

- Spring launch campaigns.
- Women-focused and education-focused observances.
- Community and environment storytelling.

### April

- Health and awareness campaigns.
- Easter and spring activity alignment.
- Content that supports family and community topics.

### May

- Labor and recognition themes.
- Family, culture, and society observances.
- Mid-year engagement boosts.

### June

- Summer opening campaigns.
- Lifestyle and leisure campaigns.
- Youth and wellness content.

### July

- Seasonal engagement and food-themed content.
- Tourism, travel, and entertainment narratives.
- Mid-summer brand activation.

### August

- Local pride and national date observances.
- Community and social awareness posts.
- Back-to-school preparation content.

### September

- Education and productivity campaigns.
- Wellness and cultural observances.
- Q4 planning and soft-launch teasers.

### October

- Fall and awareness-heavy campaigns.
- Thematic campaigns around global observance days.
- Seasonal creative and experiential content.

### November

- Gratitude and recognition campaigns.
- National independence and social awareness topics.
- Offer and commerce-focused campaigns.

### December

- End-of-year celebrations and recap content.
- Holiday season campaigns and greetings.
- New-year preparation and planning posts.

## Implementation Notes

- Keep a yearly editable planning sheet linked to this page.
- Confirm moving-date holidays (religious/lunar) every year.
- Assign campaign owners per month and observance type.
