
# Lebanese Official Holidays Reference

This page tracks Lebanese official holidays relevant to planning content, client communication, and publishing windows.

## Planning Use Cases

- Campaign scheduling and greeting posts.
- Team capacity and timeline planning.
- Market-specific campaign localization.

## Annual Validation Requirement

Lebanese official holidays can change annually due to moon-sighting and ministry updates. Confirm all dates before final schedule lock.

## Reference Source

- Official listing reference and historical source context are maintained internally in this repository.

## Recommended Operational Workflow

- Validate the official list in Q4 for the next year.
- Update internal campaign calendar and planning sheets.
- Notify account and content teams of any date changes.
- Mark flexible holidays as provisional until confirmed.
