# General Operations Highlights

These highlights define expected behavior and execution standards across operational roles.

## Client Communication and Responsiveness

- Contact clients through approved channels (for example WhatsApp groups and official email).
- Keep emails clear, structured, and concise.
- Reply to clients quickly and professionally.
- Request a clear brief before starting any project.
- Share ideas early and secure approval before major execution.

## Team Coordination and Ownership

- Keep all client information updated in approved collaboration platforms.
- Ask when requirements are unclear.
- Collect feedback from teammates and collaborate actively.
- Align with account managers before accepting additional unplanned scope.

## Delivery and Prioritization

- Respect deadlines and identify high-priority tasks.
- Plan ahead and maintain personal execution notes.
- Treat quality and consistency as non-negotiable standards.

## Design and Production Quality Notes

- Social media artwork is not print-ready by default.
- Use CMYK and print-ready settings for print deliverables.
- Use high-quality source assets only.
- Organize files and layer naming for maintainability.
- Review grammar, spelling, and visual details before delivery.

## Date and Naming Rule

Use consistent date-based naming for files and folders:

- Format: YYYY-MM-DD
- Example: 2026-07-02
