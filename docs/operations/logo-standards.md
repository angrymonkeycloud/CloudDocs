# Logo Standards

Logo design must be simple, modern, and aligned with brand identity principles.

## Design Requirements

- Keep logo construction clean and scalable.
- Use Pantone-aligned color strategy when required by brand scope.
- Present logo in full-color and one-color variants.

## Delivery Requirements

- Provide logo presentation in PDF format.
- Include font names used in the logo system.
- Include color codes used for final versions.

## Process Note

Follow the internal logo process documentation before final approval.
