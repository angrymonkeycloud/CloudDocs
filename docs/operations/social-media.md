# Social Media Operations Standard

This page defines platform operations, content direction, and production standards for social media management.

## Account Management Basics

- Maintain complete and updated account profiles.
- Keep naming consistency across platforms where possible.
- Ensure Instagram and Facebook integration is correctly configured.
- Keep profile visuals optimized and current.
- Monitor comments and messages daily.

## Content Planning and Direction

- Build content around business goals and customer needs.
- Mix formats: informational, promotional, interactive, and social proof.
- Include campaign-specific observance content using the holiday section.
- Keep tone and visual identity consistent but not repetitive.

## Recommended Content Types

- Brand and service introductions.
- Problem-solution storytelling.
- Testimonials and user experiences.
- Promotions, offers, and seasonal campaigns.
- Educational tips and awareness posts.
- Interactive posts: polls, questions, and competitions.

## Creative and Technical Standards

- Feed post baseline: 1080 x 1080 px at 72 dpi.
- Alternative feed sizes: 1080 x 1350 px or 1080 x 608 px.
- Story size: 1080 x 1920 px.
- Use high-quality imagery and maintain brand identity.
- Run final proofreading before publishing.

## Performance and Reporting

- Track campaign performance and interaction trends.
- Ensure paid boosts are active when scheduled.
- Share monthly reports with clients.
- Use insights to refine future content strategy.

## Operational Rule

Client success is the primary success metric for the team.
